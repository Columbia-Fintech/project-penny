Project Penny

Project Penny is an application that leverages OCR technology to scan Kroger brand receipts and present price comparisons at nearby stores.

This application was designed with the intention of saving users money, which is more important than ever, given the rising inflation in the US. Through offering users the ability to compare the prices of items they purchase to the prices of the same items at different stores, we hope to help them make more informed decisions and benefit their wallets.

Brands

Project Penny works with Kroger and Kroger brand receipts.

Compatible stores include: City Market, Dillons, Food 4 Less, Gerbes, Mariano's, Metro Market, Pick'n Save, Pay-Less Super Markets, Ralphs, and more!
